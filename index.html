About the Team

Open Horizon Labs.

Formed in 2013, OHL opened its doors as a contracting company, with the goal of earning enough money to bootstrap James and Greg through the process of creating a startup. Since then, we have been searching for both our business and our product.

We have experience in a broad range of software- and technology- related fields, from videoconferencing and telephony to SAAS scheduling and resource management software. From CPU emulation to data analysis. - Largely a significant benefit of providing custom software development as contractors for a considerable amount of time.

James and Greg at the office

What we do

VideoConferencing

We have designed and implemented, from the ground up, videoconferencing software built on top of SIP and H.323, aimed at government compliance for accessibility.

Resource Scheduling SAAS

Under the auspices of an earlier company, James created a a resource scheduling app centered around interpreters and accessibility needs. Customers and interpreting agencies.

Data Analysis

We have created an in house product to perform data analysis on numerous metrics involved with contracting and startup development.

Social Media Analysis

OHL consulted with a client creating a social media website built around senses of humor, to improve their Big Data analysis around many users and humorous media entries.

Educational software

OHL worked with an education-focused startup to create adaptive learning software targeted at K-12 students.

Upcoming

Now that OHL has completed its bootstrapping "fundraising" - we are actively searching for our own product to build and business to form!

About Us

James Tranovich

Pictured with his wife to the right, James has been hacking on various open source projects for many years. He loves rock climbing and the outdoors. He is currently on his second startup.

Greg Millam

An adventurous nomad, Greg has pursued work and life with equal tenacity, striving to achieve what he can. A registered participant for the next Tough Mudder Tahoe, Greg's drive is useful at OHL.
